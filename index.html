The webkit loopstation

This may be very temporamental on your machine. It's known to work very well with OSX devices, however

Make sure you give Chrome access to your microphone!

Click a track to arm for recording. After 8 beats of count in, the track will start recording for 8 beats.

At this point... start making music! The track will auto loop after 8 beats are done.

After this, any time you arm a track, it will start recording on beat 1 of the next set of 8
